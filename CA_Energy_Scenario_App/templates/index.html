Summary:

The Renewable Energy Generation Scenario Builder is a web mapping application being developed by CBI to assist with the planning process for renewable energy development projects. The overarching goal is to help identify areas that are suitable for renewable energy development in places where the impact to sensitive habitat is minimized. For example, the tool could be used to locate BLM parcels with a high solar resource potential, in areas that have already been substantially affected by human impacts such as agriculture, urban development, and natural resource extraction (low terrestrial intactness), while at the same time avoiding areas of high species diversity and linkage quality (high conservation value). These low-conflict zones are identified through a user-friendly interface consisting of slider-bars, checkboxes, dropdown menus, and text fields.

Instructions for Use:

  1. Define a search area by selecting from one of the ecological or administrative boundaries provided in the upper left hand side of the map. Selecting "User Defined" will allow you to define an arbitrary area of interest.
  2. Select a feature (polygon) or set of features (polygons) using the selection tools provided, or simply click on a feature of interest.
  3. Specify your search criteria using the slider bars and input fields on the right hand side of the screen
  4. Click the search button to identify areas meeting your criteria.
